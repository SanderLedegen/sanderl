Bio

I'm Sander Ledegen, a Belgian developer who is professionally active in JavaScript, Angular and other new technologies the web is dealing with these days. When I'm not fiddling with computer bits, you may find me reading a book, hitting the gym or doing quality checks on the beer at various parties — a nice excuse, if I say so myself. I also like hiking, travelling, cooking or just going out to dinner if the latter doesn't turn out to be a great success.
